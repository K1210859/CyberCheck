List of Proper Cybersecurity Measures:

1. Use strong passwords. Strong passwords are vital to good online security.
2. Control access.
3. Put up a firewall.
4. Use security software.
5. Update programs and systems regularly.
6. Monitor for intrusion.
7. Raise awareness.
